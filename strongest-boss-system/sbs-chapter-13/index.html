Adviser Li hesitated for a moment and said, “it’s a message from Ji Gang. It says that Su Xin received 80000 liang of dividends this month.”

Third master tiger threw away the silver in his hand, and the smile on his face gradually faded.

At the moment, Su Xin asks people to carry a box of silver directly to their new hall of happy forest.

The original humble virtue Gambling Hall has been decorated. Only some gambling equipment has been removed inside and a large number of tables and chairs have been replaced. The rule of flying eagle Gang is that old boss makes more money and subordinate people make more money every month.

If old boss is poor, he can only drink soup.

They have all seen the business of happy forest in this month, and the monthly money is certainly not less.

Sure enough, Su Xin went on: “you follow me, Su Xin. I won’t let you eat steamed bread and drink cold water to fight with others. This month’s monthly money is ten silver taels per person!”

As soon as the words came out, those gangster who were present almost cried out excitedly. Su Xin gave them more money than they thought.
